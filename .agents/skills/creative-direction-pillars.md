# Creative Direction Pillars

## Purpose
Keep the game's tone original, emotionally intelligent, and visually memorable without over-explaining itself.

## When To Use
- Defining new worlds, mechanics, or visual explorations
- Reviewing copy, UI, or narrative beats

## Required Checks
- Play expresses the search for spark, pulse, and becoming alive again.
- Emotion is carried by rhythm, feedback, environment, and transformation.
- The world can branch into music, drawing, building, and imagination without breaking the core loop.
- Visual choices preserve hand-drawn asymmetry and tactile charm.
- Avoid sentimental exposition and avoid generic faux-premium polish.

## Output Format
- Pillar fit
- Risks to originality
- One concrete recommendation

## Rollback / Failure Notes
If a concept feels borrowed or over-explained, strip it back to movement, response, and visual metaphor.
